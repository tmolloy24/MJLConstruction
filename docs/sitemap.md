## Page hierarchy

### Renovations overview

Acts as the main hub and links to every renovation service, the process, portfolio, service areas, FAQs, and consultation form.

### Service pages

Each service page should contain:

1. Hero and primary CTA
2. Service introduction
3. Common client problems and desired outcomes
4. Scope of work
5. Design and material considerations
6. MJL process
7. Relevant project gallery or case studies
8. Typical timeline
9. Investment guidance appropriate to that service
10. FAQs
11. Service areas
12. Final consultation CTA

### Portfolio system

The main portfolio page filters projects by:

- Service
- City
- Style

Each project should have its own case-study page containing:

- Project title and city
- Finished gallery
- Scope
- Client goals
- Constraints or challenge
- MJL solution
- Materials and notable features
- Approximate timeline
- Investment range where appropriate
- Testimonial
- Related service CTA
